About me

My name is Emmanuel Kasuti and I am a freshman at Dartmouth College from Nairobi, Kenya. Fun fact about me is that I play the saxophone and I am an artist too!

I’m currently pursuing a degree in Computer science and digital arts. With a focus on creative problem solving and innovation. I’m excited to learn more about how technology can be used to shape our world. I enjoy traveling, exploring new places and playing the saxophone. I also enjoy spending time in the kitchen, experimenting with new recipes and cooking for my friends and family.

I love a challenge.
I love to take a simple pencil, piece of paper, and eraser, and make something really beautiful. I disappear into my work for hours at a time, completely entranced.
